  • Drink water: Hydrate your body and avoid sugary drinks.

  • Manage stress: Practice mindfulness or engage in relaxing activities.

About MedWise AI

MedWise AI is an advanced health management platform that leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence to help you take control of your health. From managing your medical records securely to predicting health risks and providing personalized health insights, MedWise AI is designed to be your trusted partner in healthcare.

Key Features

User Profile and Medical Record Management: MedWise AI allows users to create profiles, store personal health data, and upload medical test reports. You can easily access and manage your medical records anytime, anywhere, ensuring that you have a streamlined healthcare experience at your fingertips.

  • Store health data securely
  • Upload and manage medical reports
  • Access your records anytime, anywhere

Image Analysis for Disease Detection: Our platform utilizes cutting-edge image analysis techniques to detect diseases such as cancer and knee osteoarthritis. By analyzing medical imaging data like MRI and CT scans, we use Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to detect patterns and assist in early diagnosis and treatment planning.

  • Analyze MRI and CT scans for disease detection
  • Uses CNNs for pattern recognition
  • Early detection and treatment assistance

Disease Prediction: MedWise AI utilizes Support Vector Machines (SVM) to predict diseases based on user input. By providing early risk assessments, the platform helps you understand potential health risks and take preventive measures.

  • AI-driven disease prediction
  • Early risk assessments for health concerns
  • Data-driven health insights

Medical Chatbot: MedWise AI features a medical chatbot powered by LangChain, OpenAI, and Groq. This chatbot provides real-time medical support, answering questions related to healthcare and offering personalized advice based on user inputs.

  • Instant responses to health queries
  • Real-time medical advice
  • Powered by LangChain, OpenAI, and Groq
