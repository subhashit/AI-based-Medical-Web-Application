5 Tips for a Healthier Life
  • Eat a balanced diet: Include a variety of fruits, vegetables, and lean proteins.
  • Stay active: Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise most days of the week.
  • Drink water: Hydrate your body and avoid sugary drinks.
  • Get enough sleep: Prioritize sleep to help the body recover and stay energized.
  • Manage stress: Practice mindfulness or engage in relaxing activities.

About MedWise AI

MedWise AI is an advanced health management platform that leverages the power of Artificial Intelligence to help you take control of your health. From managing your medical records securely to predicting health risks and providing personalized health insights, MedWise AI is designed to be your trusted partner in healthcare.

User Profile and Medical Record Management

MedWise AI allows users to create profiles, store personal health data, and upload medical test reports. You can easily access and manage your medical records anytime, anywhere, ensuring that you have a streamlined healthcare experience at your fingertips.

Image Analysis for Disease Detection

Our platform utilizes cutting-edge image analysis techniques to detect diseases such as cancer and knee osteoarthritis. By analyzing medical imaging data like MRI and CT scans, we use Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to detect patterns and assist in early diagnosis and treatment planning.

Disease Prediction

MedWise AI utilizes Support Vector Machines (SVM) to predict diseases based on user input. By providing early risk assessments, the platform helps you understand potential health risks and take preventive measures.

Medical Chatbot

MedWise AI features a medical chatbot powered by LangChain, OpenAI, and Groq. This chatbot provides real-time medical support, answering questions related to healthcare and offering personalized advice based on user inputs.
